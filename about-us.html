About Renewed Mind Leaders

We reach & serve the purpose of Empowering the leaders or tomorrow.
Basically, the Renewed Mind Leaders is an organization founded by God, through the President Ambassador Ngassa Merlin. The vision is to raise a generation of purpose driven youths, who are mentally sound, physically healthy, financially buoyant and God fearing, having a strong sense of integrity, demonstrating visionary leadership.

Brief History of R.M.L

As a first year student in the University of Buea in 2012, a 19 year old young man who just entered the university as a “freshman” as they called it was very uncomfortable with the manner of life of his fellow peers who were very unconcerned about their future, and when he approach some of his peers to find out why they were so passive in a very active stage of their lives he was vehemently ignored by them, while others saw him as a distractor to their career of scoring good grades and graduating from the universit

This young teenager became very disturbed on why should a student be at the tertiary level of school without discovering their purpose in life or taking the responsibility to be an exemplary role model for others to follow,
this obsession led the former senior prefect boy of CCAS (Cameroon College of Arts and Science) Kumba Ngassa Merlin Colonel to come up with a forum where young people can be inspire to discover their purpose in life and how they can take up the challenge of bringing creative and innovative solutions to some of the problems faced by the society. That forum grew up to become an organization called Renewed Mind Leaders which is a leading young leaders Network in Cameroon that has impacted over 50,000 youths in 20 secondary schools,4 higher Institutions ,3 vocational institutions and have carried out other strategic leadership and mentorship programs around the university mileu, communities and towns as a whole in Cameroon. It has had in attendance Senior Government Officials like the Lord Mayor, Magistrates, Deans, Professors, Entrepreneurs and other well renowned stake holders in the nation.
